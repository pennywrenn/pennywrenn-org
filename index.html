Content Strategy

Most content consultants know either the marketing mechanics or the editorial quality, and they deliver accordingly. Penny Wrenn works from both ends simultaneously — the SEM infrastructure that makes content findable, and the editorial rigor that makes it worth finding. These are not separate disciplines. They are the same discipline applied at different scales.

The practice is built on a decade of search engine marketing, content production, and editorial work across industries — including health technology through TeamTioga, where the stakes of getting information right are immediate and concrete. The same precision that health-tech content demands transfers directly to any communications challenge where accuracy, clarity, and findability all matter at once.

The framework is called Computational Authorship: the deliberate use of digital tools and data to produce content that is genuinely authored — not generated, not templated, not optimized within an inch of its life. Content that a human being wrote, with a human being's understanding of what another human being actually needs.

Health-Tech Experience

TeamTioga: Where Content Precision

Health technology is a sector where content inaccuracy has direct human consequences. The experience of producing content strategy for health-tech through TeamTioga — navigating the intersection of medical accuracy, regulatory requirements, patient literacy, and digital findability — is a master class in the disciplines that matter in every content environment.

The standards that health-tech demands — verify everything, simplify without distorting, reach the audience that needs the information, not just the audience that's easy to reach — are the standards that distinguish good content strategy from the kind that fills space without accomplishing anything.

Computational Authorship

"The most powerful content tool is still a human being
who understands what another human being needs."

Computational Authorship is the framework for bringing together the scale and precision of digital content production with the irreducible quality of genuine editorial judgment. It is not AI — or rather, it is not AI in the sense of outsourcing the thinking. It is the strategic use of every available tool in service of content that a human being wrote, for a human being who needs it.

Natural language is not input. It is testimony. Every search query, every piece of content, every keyword is evidence of something a human being cares about. Computational Authorship treats that evidence with the seriousness it deserves.

This is what Penny Wrenn Consulting offers: the intelligence to understand what people actually need, and the craft to provide it at scale.
